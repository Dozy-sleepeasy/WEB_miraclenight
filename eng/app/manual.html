Sleep

Here's what you can do when you're sleeping!

STEP 01
Set to inaudible frequency

If you're uncomfortable listening to Monaural Beats or if your sleeping partner doesn't like the sound of Monaural Beats, try turning on the inaudible frequency button!

If you're not comfortable listening to Monaural Beats, you can add white noise to hear them together.

STEP 03
Pause button can stop the sound temporary

- If something unexpected happens and you need to stop sleeping, hit the "snooze" button in the top left corner

If you need to wake up earlier than your setted wake-up time, press the X button in the top right corner to end your sleep

End Sleep

When you end sleep, you can see your sleep data!

- You can see when you went to bed, when you woke up and how long you slept
- You can see others in your Wake-up room
- You can view your sleep data by clicking the 'Calendar button' in the top right corner of the home screen.
