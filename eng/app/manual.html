Prepare Sleep

You need to be fully prepared for the best sleep! If you press [Start] on the Home screen, you can prepare three things for sleep

STEP 01
Set the Wake-up time

When you set the wake-up time, the total sleep time is also automatically calculated for you

STEP 03
Find the best volume for your environment

Based on the noise decibel of your surrounding environment, we find you the optimal Monaural Beats volume! You can set the volume according to your preference

üåôÔ∏è Tip!

STEP 04
Listen to the Sleep guide

Listen to the Sleep Guide written by Psychologist in Yonsei University

STEP 05
Place the device at the right position

1) Place the device about 30cm away from the ear. 30cm is about the length of a4 paper.

2) Charge the battery sufficiently. If you use Miracle Night all night long with low battery, the device can heat up.

During Sleep

What you can do during sleep?

STEP 01
You can pause if any unexpected event happens

1) Press the Pause button on the upper left when you need to stop sleeping due to an unexpected situation

2) If you want to return to the sleep screen after a pause, press the [Resume] button

üåôÔ∏è Tip!

Please note that if the app is in the background for a long time, it may be terminate

STEP 02
If you wake up, you can end sleep

If you have to wake up earlier than the time you set up, press the X button on the upper right to end the sleep

üåôÔ∏è Tip!
