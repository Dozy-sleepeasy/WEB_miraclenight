Subscription Info

Miracle Night's subscription service provides unlimited access to all content during the selected period. The subscription fee is automatically paid on the same day every month or year with the App Store account. You can unsubscribe or renew your subscription through Subscription Management in your App Store account 24 hours prior to the next payment date. Refunds and other processing procedures follow the Terms of Service.

Cancel subscription

If you are an Apple user, you can unsubscribe at [App Store > Account > Subscribe].

If you are an Android user, you can subscribe at [Google Play Store > Account > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions].

Even if you cancel the subscription, you can still use the service for the remaining time.

Refund unwanted subscription

If you don't want to subscribe, you can cancel your subscription at any time up to 24 hours prior to the subscription renewal date. Refunds and other purchase-related procedures for already paid passes follow the terms of service and store policy. Please contact the in-app customer center or store for any unresolved issues.

OS change

Google Play Store and App Store cannot share subscription licenses, so if your OS system changes to another model, you must cancel the existing subscription and subscribe to a new one at the new store. At that time, the price of the existing subscription pass and the price of the new subscription pass may differ depending on the price policy change.
