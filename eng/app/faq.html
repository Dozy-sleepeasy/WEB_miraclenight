1. Monaural Beats

Q. What are Monaural Beats?

Monaural Beats are a combination of sound waves that are combined to synchronize your brain waves to a specific frequency. Miracle Night takes the ordinary Monaural Beats to the next level and provides you with layered Monaural Beats that are optimized for sleep induction. The patented algorithm can only be heard at Miracle Night.

Q. How do Monaural Beats induce sleep?

The human brain produces five different types of brain waves: Gamma (30-100 Hz), Beta (12-30 Hz), Alpha (8-12 Hz), Ceta (4-8 Hz), Delta (0.5-4 Hz), in total. We emit brainwaves between alpha and delta when we sleep, and the Monaural Beats induce deep sleep by synchronizing our brainwaves to the brainwaves of the sleep state.

Q. How effective are Monaural Beats?

As a result of several experiments and clinical trials in collaboration with Yonsei University's Applied Brain Cognitive Science Institute, the delta wave of subjects increased by about 16% on average within 10 minutes. One subject also experienced a 28% increase in delta waves in just five minutes.

Q. Are there any side-effects?

Several studies have shown that Monaural Beats are not at risk of any kind. However, keeping the device too close to you or increasing the volume can cause hearing issues, so please follow the volume guide provided by the service!
For more information on Monaural Beats, refer to the article [Science of Monaural Beats].

2. Volume

Q. What is the optimal volume setting?

It has been found that Monaural Beats can induce sleep when it sounds about 20 dB or more to the user. You can think of 20 dB as a very small, whispering sound. According to studies, Monaural Beats are effective in a range of areas ranging from about 20 dB to 75 dB.

Q. I can't hear the sound set to the recommended volume. Should I raise it?

If the surrounding environment is very quiet (around 20 dB) but you cannot hear the sound well, this may be due to an individual's hearing condition. Even if the sensitivity to sound is low or hearing is poor, the effect on brain waves is the same, so it is recommended that you do not increase the volume.

Q. The recommended volume is too loud. Can I decrease it?

If the volume is too loud, there is a possibility that your hearing may be impaired or your sleep can be disturbed. We recommend that you set it up with a low voice that is almost as low as possible.

Q. Can I charge while using the app?

We recommend that you use it while charging it. If the battery is low, it can cause heat up and fast battery consumption at the same time, so please use it while charging it so that it doesn't burden the device.
